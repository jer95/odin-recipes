Teriyaki Chicken

Simple Teriyaki Chicken recipe

Get ready for a tasty homemade teriyaki chicken with a healthy twist! Cook the chicken until it's browned, and make a delicious sauce with soy sauce, honey, vinegar, garlic, and ginger. While the chicken cooks, prepare some fresh broccoli by steaming or stir-frying it until it's tender-crisp. The teriyaki sauce adds a wonderful flavor to the chicken and broccoli. Serve it all over rice or noodles for a satisfying and nutritious meal. Enjoy the savory, sweet, and nutritious goodness!

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless,skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey or brown sugar
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil(for cooking)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips or bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a bowl,combine soy sauce,honey or brown sugar,sesame oil,chopped garlic,and grated ginger.Mix well to create the teriayki sauce.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet or pan over medium heat. Add a small amount of sesame oil to the skillet.
  4. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until it is browned and cooked through, approximately 6-8 minutes. Stir occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  5. Pour the teriyaki sauce over the cooked chicken in the skillet. Stir to coat the chicken evenly with the sauce. Continue cooking for an additional 1-2 minutes, allowing the sauce to heat through and slightly thicken.
  6. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  7. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Add a pinch of salt to the boiling water.
  8. Add the broccoli florets to the boiling water and cook for approximately 3-4 minutes or until the broccoli is tender-crisp. Be careful not to overcook the broccoli.
  9. Drain the cooked broccoli,and rinse it briefly with cold water to stop the cooking process.
  10. Drain the cooked broccoli,and rinse it briefly with cold water to stop the cooking process.
  11. Toss the chicken and broccoli together until they are evenly mixed and coated with the teriyaki sauce.

Enjoy your homemade teriyaki chicken with broccoli!
