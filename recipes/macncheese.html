Baked Mac & Cheese

Simple mac & cheese recipe

Ultimate comfort food: Baked Mac and Cheese. Creamy, cheesy perfection with a golden, crispy topping.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (about 8 ounces or 225 grams) elbow macaroni or any other pasta shape you prefer
  • 2 tablespoons (30 grams) butter
  • 2 tablespoons (16 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (480 milliliters) milk
  • 2 cups (200 grams) shredded cheddar cheese (or a combination of your favorite cheeses)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup (25 grams) breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika (optional, for added flavor)
