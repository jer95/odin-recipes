Baked Mac & Cheese

Simple mac & cheese recipe

Ultimate comfort food: Baked Mac and Cheese. Creamy, cheesy perfection with a golden, crispy topping.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (about 8 ounces or 225 grams) elbow macaroni or any other pasta shape you prefer
  • 2 tablespoons (30 grams) butter
  • 2 tablespoons (16 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (480 milliliters) milk
  • 2 cups (200 grams) shredded cheddar cheese (or a combination of your favorite cheeses)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup (25 grams) breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika (optional, for added flavor)
  • Optional add-ins:Cooked bacon, diced ham, sautéed onions, chopped cooked vegetables, or any other ingredients you like to include for extra flavor and texture.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray.
  2. Cook about 2 cups of elbow macaroni according to the instructions on the package until they are cooked but still firm. Drain the cooked macaroni and set it aside.
  3. In a medium-sized pot, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Once melted, add 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour to the pot and stir it for about 1 minute.
  4. Gradually pour 2 cups of milk into the pot while stirring constantly. Continue stirring until the mixture thickens slightly and comes to a gentle simmer.
  5. Remove the pot from the heat and add 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese to the mixture. Stir until the cheese is fully melted and the sauce becomes smooth.
  6. Season the cheese sauce with 1/2 teaspoon of salt, 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper, and 1/4 teaspoon of paprika. Stir well to combine the flavors.
  7. Combine the cooked macaroni and the cheese sauce in a large bowl, ensuring all the macaroni is coated with the sauce.
  8. Transfer the macaroni and cheese mixture into the greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.
  9. Sprinkle about 1/4 cup of breadcrumbs over the top of the macaroni and cheese.
  10. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for around 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  11. Once baked, remove the dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

    12. Enjoy your delicious homemade baked mac and cheese!
